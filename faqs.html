Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about admissions, academic requirements, bank transfers, or registration? Find quick answers right here.

Your unique application ID (structured as BOUESTI/McDES/2026/XXXXX) is automatically generated by the database once you submit your admission form on our application portal [1].

Keep this number secure. You will need it to search your application status on the Admissions Checker tab, download your printable application slip, and create your student login account [1].

Requirements vary based on your mode of entry [1]:

  • O'Level Entry: Five (5) credit passes in SSCE/GCE/NECO/NABTEB including English and Mathematics at not more than two sittings [1].
  • Direct Entry: Minimum of Merit/Credit in at least two subjects in OND/NCE/A-Levels plus five O'level credits as listed above [1].

All academic lectures and exams for the Directorate of Part-Time Programmes are held at our designated **Ibadan Study Centre** [1]:

Bishop Phillips Academy, Iwo-Road, Ibadan [1].

Our academic programs operate on a **Weekend Part-Time** structure. Lectures hold physically every Saturday and Sunday to accommodate working class professionals [1].

All academic degrees, exam transcripts, and final graduation certificates are **fully issued directly by the Senate of Bamidele Olumilua University of Education, Science and Technology, Ikere-Ekiti (BOUESTI)** [1].

The academic degrees awarded are exactly the same as those earned by main-campus students, carrying full accreditation by the National Universities Commission (NUC).

We do not accept raw credit card details on our site [1]. Instead, you must make a direct bank transfer to our verified bank account [1]:

After paying, you must submit your transaction receipt ID and upload a photo/PDF of your bank receipt on your student portal **Pay All Fees** page [1]. Finally, tap the **Send to WhatsApp** button to log it directly with our registry desk for verification and approval [1].

Yes, installment and part-payments are fully supported to provide financial flexibility [1].

When settling your fees on the **Pay All Fees** panel, you can choose "Custom Installment" and type in any amount you wish to transfer (provided it does not exceed your outstanding balance) [1]. Your student dashboard automatically subtracts the paid sum and updates your outstanding balance statement live [1].
